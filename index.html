Study Overview

This supplemental webpage includes detailed methodological visuals and comprehensive datasets from our comparative study examining the pressure-volume characteristics of endotracheal tube (ETT) cuffs across rigid, elastic, and cadaveric airway models.

Main Article (submitted): Characterization of Endotracheal Tube Cuff Pressure-Volume in Simulated and Cadaveric Tracheas
