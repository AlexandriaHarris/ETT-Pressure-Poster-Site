Study Overview

This supplemental webpage provides detailed visual aids and comprehensive datasets from our investigation into the pressure-volume characteristics of endotracheal tube cuffs. The study systematically compared cuff performance across rigid, elastic, and cadaveric airway models, highlighting the critical role airway compliance and cuff design play in clinical airway management.
This page provides supplementary materials and extended data referenced in our manuscript. Please note that all methodology and findings here are intended to complement the primary publication. For any questions, contact: harrisa8@upmc.edu.
